Get in Touch

Welcome to PrintPerfect support! We're dedicated to providing you with the best possible experience and assistance. Whether you're experiencing technical difficulties, have suggestions for new features, need help optimizing your prints, or simply want to share feedback about your experience, we're here to listen and help.

Our support team typically responds to inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For faster assistance with common issues, we recommend checking our comprehensive FAQ section first, where you'll find detailed answers to frequently asked questions about DPI, print quality, color modes, and troubleshooting common problems.

When to Contact Us

Technical Support: If you're experiencing bugs, errors, or unexpected behavior while using our print adjuster tool, please contact us with details about the issue, including your browser version, operating system, and steps to reproduce the problem.

Feature Requests: We love hearing from users about features they'd like to see! If you have ideas for improving PrintPerfect, whether it's a new adjustment option, additional format support, or workflow enhancements, please share your suggestions.

Print Quality Issues: If your prints aren't turning out as expected, we can help you troubleshoot. Describe the issue (pixelation, color shifts, sizing problems, etc.) and include information about your source image, target paper size, and print settings.

General Inquiries: For questions about how to use specific features, best practices for different printing scenarios, or general print optimization advice, we're happy to provide guidance.

Business Inquiries: If you're interested in partnerships, integrations, or using PrintPerfect for commercial purposes, please reach out to discuss opportunities.

What to Include in Bug Reports

To help us resolve technical issues quickly, please include the following information when reporting bugs:

  • Browser and Version: Chrome 120, Firefox 121, Safari 17, etc.
  • Operating System: Windows 11, macOS Sonoma, iOS 17, etc.
  • Steps to Reproduce: Detailed list of actions that trigger the issue
  • Expected Behavior: What you expected to happen
  • Actual Behavior: What actually happened
  • Console Errors: Any error messages in browser console (F12)
  • File Information: Type, size, and resolution of images causing issues
  • Screenshots: Visual reference of the problem (if applicable)
